## 2. Description of services

Warmvoice is an assistive communication tool that mainly provides:

- Local features such as large-type display, copy, and clear screen  
- Offline TTS and offline STT (on-device via system capabilities)  
- Online speech features via **Baidu AI Cloud** speech SDKs (**membership required**; within published monthly quotas the App prefers online processing; when quotas are exhausted or you are not a member, offline processing is used automatically)  
- LAN friends / messaging (via iOS local-network capabilities)  

We may upgrade, iterate, or adjust services. If a change materially affects your use, we will try to notify you in a reasonable manner.

### 4.3 Friend chat content and reporting

When using LAN friend chat, you must ensure messages are lawful and respectful. You must not send content involving pornography, violence, terrorism, gambling, fraud, or other illegal or non-compliant material. The App performs **on-device** rule- and format-based filtering; inappropriate content may fail to send or may be shown as a placeholder. You may long-press a message to **report inappropriate content** by email, and you may **remove a friend** to stop communicating with that peer. Because chat is peer-to-peer over LAN, we cannot intercept what the other device sends; we can only apply display and storage handling on **this** device.

## 5. Online speech and third-party services

When you are a **member** and use **online** speech recognition or **online** speech synthesis **within** the **monthly quotas** shown under “Membership & subscription”, the App may send necessary audio or text to Baidu AI Cloud for processing to return recognized text or synthesized speech. Third-party handling is governed by Baidu’s service agreements and privacy policy.

When you are **not a member**, or you have **used up** the applicable online quota for the month, the App automatically uses offline recognition or offline synthesis (where device capabilities allow), and generally **does not** transmit the above online-processing content to Baidu. You may manage related capabilities via system permissions or optional in-App settings where available.

Reference links (subject to Baidu’s official pages):

- Baidu AI Cloud privacy policy: `https://cloud.baidu.com/doc/Agreements/s/Plr0fi68q`  
- Baidu AI Cloud user agreement: `https://cloud.baidu.com/doc/Agreements/s/yjwvy1x03`

## 6. Membership and subscriptions (auto-renewal)

### 6.1 Membership benefits

Membership mainly unlocks online speech recognition and online speech synthesis and may include larger phrase storage, etc. **Specific benefits are as shown on the in-App “Membership & subscription” screen.**

In the current version, members receive **separate** monthly quotas for online speech recognition and online speech synthesis, each **1000 uses per calendar month**; counters **reset at 00:00 on the 1st of each month**. After either monthly quota is exhausted, the corresponding feature **automatically switches** to offline recognition or offline synthesis; you may notice differences in speed or quality.

Quota numbers may change with product iterations; the latest in-App display prevails.

### 6.2 Products and pricing

Tiers, list prices, and promotions are as **shown in the App Store purchase UI in real time**.

### 6.3 Auto-renewal (important)

1) Subscriptions are **auto-renewing** unless you turn off auto-renewal in Apple ID subscription settings **at least 24 hours** before the end of the current period.  
2) Apple will charge your App Store account within **24 hours before** renewal.  
3) You can manage or cancel subscriptions in iOS Apple ID subscription settings.  
4) We cannot cancel subscriptions on your behalf; after cancellation, you retain membership benefits for the period already paid until it ends.  

### 6.4 Restore purchases

After changing devices or reinstalling, you may use “Restore purchases” so Apple can return subscription state and restore entitlements where applicable.

### 6.5 Refunds

Subscription refunds and disputes are generally handled by Apple under its policies. You may request refunds through Apple’s official process.
