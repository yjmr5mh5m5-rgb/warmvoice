# Warmvoice Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 12, 2026  
Last updated: May 12, 2026  

Welcome to **Warmvoice** (this “App”). The App and related services are provided by the party responsible for development and operation ( “we”, “us”). We take your personal information and privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

If you do not agree with any part of this Policy, please stop using the App.

---

## 1. Scope

This Policy applies when you use the App on an iOS device, including but not limited to:

- Local features such as large-type display, copy, and clear screen  
- Offline text-to-speech (TTS) and offline speech recognition (STT) (processed on-device via system capabilities)  
- Online speech features provided by **Baidu AI Cloud** speech SDKs (recognition and synthesis; **membership required**, subject to monthly quotas published in the App; when you are not a member or quotas are exhausted, offline processing is preferred and **corresponding content is not sent to Baidu** for online processing)  
- LAN friends / messaging (using iOS local-network capabilities)  
- Membership subscriptions (Apple StoreKit)  

---

## 2. Information we collect or process

### 2.1 No accounts; no proprietary backend

The App **does not require you to register an account**. We currently **do not operate our own servers/backend** to store your text, voice, or chat content.  
When you are **not a member**, or you are a member but have **used up** the applicable **monthly online speech quota** and therefore use offline recognition/synthesis, related voice and text are generally processed **on the device** and **are not** transmitted to Baidu AI Cloud for online processing. Only when you are a **member within quota** and use the **online** recognition/synthesis path may necessary content be sent to the third party.

### 2.1.1 No crash analytics (current)

As of this Policy’s last updated date, the App **does not integrate third-party crash reporting or analytics SDKs**, and we do not collect your usage analytics for statistical purposes. If this changes in the future, we will update this Policy and notify you by appropriate means.

### 2.2 Information stored locally on your device (under your control)

To provide features, the App may store the following on your device (e.g. via iOS local storage / databases):

- Phrase content, ordering, pins, and recent-use state  
- Friends and chat messages (text produced by LAN communication)  
- Membership / subscription display state (e.g. active/inactive, restore results)  
- Settings and preferences (e.g. theme, font size, speech rate and pitch; **monthly usage counters** for online recognition and synthesis (calendar month, typically reset on the 1st of each month), etc.)  

**Friend chat moderation (device only):** To reduce illegal, non-compliant, or harmful content risk, the App performs rule- and format-based checks on friend chat text **on your device**; we **do not** upload your chat content to **our** servers for this purpose. The current version also **does not** call third-party **cloud text moderation** services; if we add this later, we will update this Policy and describe the third party and purposes.

By default this information stays on your device. You may remove local data by deleting the App; we also recommend that you manage backups and permissions at the system level.

### 2.3 Third-party processing for online speech (Baidu AI Cloud)

When you use **online** speech recognition or **online** speech synthesis **during an active membership** and **within** the **monthly quotas** described under “Membership & subscription” in the App, the App may send necessary content to Baidu AI Cloud speech services:

- Online STT: **audio** may be uploaded to obtain recognized text  
- Online TTS: **text** you input or display may be uploaded to obtain synthesized speech  

SDKs we use:

- Baidu AI Cloud speech recognition SDK  
- Baidu AI Cloud speech synthesis SDK  

**Quotas and fallback:** Online recognition and online synthesis each have monthly limits and calendar-month reset rules (see in-App display for exact numbers). After either monthly quota is exhausted, the corresponding feature **automatically uses offline processing**, and **such requests are not sent to Baidu**. Non-members primarily use offline capabilities.

**Important:**  
1) You can manage microphone and related permissions in system settings; the App may also offer optional toggles for certain features where applicable. Regardless of path, **when you are not a member or quotas are exhausted, recognition/synthesis is primarily offline** and generally does **not** trigger online transmission to Baidu.  
2) Baidu’s storage, use, and protection of processed data are governed by Baidu’s privacy policy, SDK terms, and service agreements.  
3) We do not intentionally upload your online speech content to **our own** servers for storage (the App has no proprietary backend storage).  

**Reference links (subject to Baidu’s official pages):**  
- Baidu AI Cloud privacy policy: `https://cloud.baidu.com/doc/Agreements/s/Plr0fi68q`  
- Baidu AI Cloud user agreement: `https://cloud.baidu.com/doc/Agreements/s/yjwvy1x03`  
- Speech recognition SDK privacy policy (AI open capability reference): `https://cloud.baidu.com/doc/AI_REFERENCE/s/Pm3zhy56g`

## 3. Device permissions and purposes

### 3.1 Microphone

- Purpose: speech recognition (offline/online), multi-speaker mode audio capture  
- Note: if denied, speech recognition / multi-speaker features may be unavailable  

### 3.2 Speech recognition (Speech)

- Purpose: offline speech recognition (on-device via system Speech)  
- Note: if denied, offline speech recognition may be unavailable (you may still type with the keyboard)  

### 3.3 Local network

- Purpose: discover friends on LAN, establish connections, send/receive messages, multi-user communication  
- Note: if denied, LAN friends and related features may be unavailable

## 4. Membership subscriptions and payments (Apple)

Membership subscriptions are provided through Apple’s App Store and StoreKit. We do **not** directly collect your bank card or similar payment details.  
Purchases, renewals, cancellations, and refunds in the App Store are handled by Apple under its rules. We only receive information necessary to determine whether membership entitlements are active and to display subscription status in the App.

## 5. Sharing, transfer, and public disclosure

### 5.1 Sharing

Except as described below, we do not share your personal information with any company, organization, or individual:

- When you are a member using **online** speech recognition or **online** speech synthesis **within** the published monthly quotas: to complete processing, necessary data is transmitted to Baidu AI Cloud speech services  
- When required by laws, regulations, or competent judicial or administrative authorities  

### 5.2 Transfer and public disclosure

We do not transfer or publicly disclose your personal information unless we have your explicit consent or applicable laws require otherwise.

## 6. Security

We take reasonable and feasible measures to protect your information, including minimizing collection, storing data locally on device, and applying membership gates and monthly quotas for online features.  
Please understand that no system is 100% secure. Keep your device and system accounts safe, and be aware of disclosure risks when using voice features in public places.

- Turn off microphone, speech recognition, or local network permissions in system settings  
- Review quotas and reset rules on “Membership & subscription” and related screens; adjust optional in-App toggles where available  
- Delete the App to clear locally stored data (phrases, chats, settings, etc.)
